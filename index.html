Q&A with Alex Johnson

What's your pre-game routine?
I usually start with meditation, followed by a light workout and specific visualization exercises for the game ahead.
How do you stay motivated during off-season?
I set specific goals for the next season and break them down into daily tasks. Tracking progress keeps me motivated!
What's your favorite basketball memory?
Scoring the winning shot in the championship game during my college years. The crowd's energy was incredible!
